Introduction
eDuck is an academic information system whose main role is to help organize university studies - namely courses. It was created as a final project by three students (D. Kamenská, V. Borodin, T. Peregrín) from Matej Bel University in Banská Bystrica.
This guide will take you through the many useful features and functions of eDuck and will help you get better acquainted with the system.
Notable features:
  • Homepage
  • Courses
  • User Management
  • Account Management
